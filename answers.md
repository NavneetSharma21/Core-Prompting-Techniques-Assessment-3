# Core Prompting Techniques Assessment-3

## CHAIN OF THOUGHT (CoT)

### Exercise 1

Question:
Is 45 divisible by 9?

Answer:

Rule:
A number is divisible by 9 if the sum of its digits is divisible by 9.

45

4 + 5 = 9

9 is divisible by 9.

Final Answer:
Yes, 45 is divisible by 9.
